How it works

Up and reading in three steps

1

Create your account

Sign up with your student details. Your account will be reviewed and activated by a librarian — usually within the same day.

2

Find a book you love

Browse the full catalogue from your dashboard. When something catches your eye, submit a borrow request in one click.

3

Pick it up and enjoy

Once approved, collect your book from the library. Return it on time to keep your record clean and your access open.

4

Track everything online

Your dashboard shows current loans, due dates, borrow history, and any fines — all in one place, any time.
